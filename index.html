From London to Dubai

My name is Joe, and my journey into trading began with a common illusion. "Itâ€™s easy money," they said. I could already see myself in Dubai, driving a Lamborghini.

I opened my first account with 20K, and the first profits came almost instantly. What bad luck... Because in that moment, I was convinced I had cracked the code: instinct, FOMO, breakout after breakout. As the account grew, my ego doubled. I was projecting future gains and dreaming of financial freedom, certain I had found a simple path in a world that, in reality, gives no discounts to anyone.

Then, reality hit hard. Global crises began to take their toll, and faced with a trade going the wrong way, I made the most classic mistake: refusing to accept the loss and continuing to buy as the price kept dropping. Emotions had taken over. In a single night, the account was blown.

That loss proved to me that trading without a rigorous method against "entities" infinitely larger than myself was a losing battle from the start.

Instead of quitting, I decided to change my approach. I stopped trying to "guess" market movements and turned toward systematic trading. I translated my strategies into mechanical rules, eliminating any room for emotion or heat-of-the-moment interpretation.

Today, my trading is based exclusively on rigorous analysis and the statistical study of market movements, accepting that discipline and commitment are the only true competitive advantages possible.

But in the end, did I ever make it to Dubai?

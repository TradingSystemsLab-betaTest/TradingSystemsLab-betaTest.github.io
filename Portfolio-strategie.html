"Strategies portfolio."

The portfolio is updated on a monthly basis, featuring the strategies that have demonstrated the best performance.

Our portfolio is constructed through a rigorous selection process based not only on quantitative performance criteria, but also on an overall vision. The composition is based on three fundamental pillars:

1. Diversification and Synergy: We do not limit ourselves to adding up individual strategies; we select models that interact harmoniously with each other, reducing correlations and optimizing the overall risk balance.

2. Historical and Prospective Performance: We analyze past returns to identify the strategies that have demonstrated greater resilience and consistency in results.

3. Psychological Sustainability: This is our distinctive feature. Each strategy is evaluated to ensure that volatility levels and drawdowns are compatible with the investor's peace of mind, fostering balanced emotional management over the long term.
